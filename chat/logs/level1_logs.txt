Q: What are the colors in a rainbow?
A: Step 1:  A rainbow is a meteorological phenomenon.

Step 2:  Rainbows are caused by sunlight refracting (bending) through water droplets in the air.

Step 3: This refraction separates the sunlight into its constituent colors.

Step 4: The colors in a rainbow, typically listed in order, are: Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, and Violet.  These are often remembered using the acronym ROY G. BIV.
